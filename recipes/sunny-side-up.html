Sunny Side Up

Description

Fried eggs are usually thought of as diner breakfast fare, but they’re a easy way to add protein to all kinds of meals— salads, sandwiches, burgers or in a classic Huevos Rancheros. There a four basic styles of fried eggs—sunny side up, over easy, over medium and over well—indicating the level of doneness.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 large egg
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. In a small nonstick over medium-low heat, heat oil until shimmering. Crack egg into pan. Cook 3 to 4 minutes, or until white is opaque and the edges are just beginning to brown. Remove from pan and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Serve with your favorite breakfast sides.
